Welcome to Pro Gravel Calculator Online

Welcome to Pro Gravel Calculator, the industry-standard tool designed for homeowners, contractors, and landscape architects who demand precision in their material estimation.

Our Mission

In the construction world, we know that "close enough" isn't good enough. Under-ordering leads to project delays and costly delivery fees, while over-ordering results in wasted budget and disposal headaches. Our mission is to eliminate that guesswork.

Why Trust Us? (E-E-A-T)

This tool was developed with direct input from civil engineers and hardscaping professionals.

  • Expertise: Our density formulas are based on standard aggregate specific gravities used in civil engineering (e.g., 2,835 lbs/yd³ for crushed stone).
  • Experience: We understand real-world site conditions, which is why we emphasize the "Waste Factor" rule.
  • Authority: We are a trusted resource for quickly estimating tons, yards, and bags for driveways, patios, and drainage projects.
